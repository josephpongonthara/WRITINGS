you're supposed to have fun

Staring into the ceiling mirrors at Pub, I began to think about how fitting it would be to film a movie here. 3 years spent, 1 in my room, the 2nd in a cage & the 3rd finally free. I went to college, I served my time, I am a bird released from captivity, and I’m supposed to enjoy it.

I’d be a liar if I said it hasn’t been a blast. Receiving attention (wanted & unwanted), the spontaneous eye contact & smile, forming relationships (wanted & unwanted) & of course, getting drunk.

Like a fever dream, the loop restarts, I’ve re-entered the same simulation I’ve been abusing for the past 8 months. Will I ever stop, no. As I’ve said before, I’m a sucker for drama.

Game resumes. Holy shit, I’m here again. My blink time doubles, as expected, but wow there are so many people. Like a drug the enjoyment of walking into a packed bar is exhilarating no matter how many times it’s been done before. However, sobriety always kicks in at some point and I realize that I’ve seen this a million times.

Robot-like I ignore my rational thoughts, and plug back into my role as a jester in a crowded circus filled with people who deep down are feeling the same way I am. Now I begin going through the same levels I’ve completed a thousand times over. ATM, Wait at the bar, 1 Whiskey Sour 2 Pub Bombs, Round of hellos, go to the bathroom, drink from flask, fake a phone call, drink from flask again, NOW, the night’s begun.

Feeling somewhat accomplished, I’m finally ready to socialize. I’ve bought into the illusion, I’m going to have a mind-numbing, meaningless, dopamine filled, entertaining night. The ecstasy is prematurely kicking in, fuck it, I’m ready for fireworks; however, an unfamiliar face appears. Wait! It’s familiar, “how are you doing” the face says, with the liquid courage I’ve injected into my veins I decide to speak my mind, I say, to be honest I want to leave. She replies “why would you leave, you’re drunk and at Pub, you’re supposed to have fun here.”

Angels appear in the strangest forms. Tonight, she was my angel. The chains were broken, the game began to glitch. In that split second I realized, fuck this game. I’m leaving.
