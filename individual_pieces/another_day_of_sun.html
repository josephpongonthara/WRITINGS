trivial thoughts, tenets & tales

another day of sun

Juxtaposition.

My townhome sits atop hills in either direction.

The safest place is the one I despise.

Although I am a man fearful of everything, most comfortable in stability, the more it continues only adds to my pile of misery.

Little joys, that’s what I look for.

I’ll forever dream of the eyes on the French girl who never was today.

My cowardice on full display, an opportunity of a lifetime gone.

The subway giveth and it also taketh.

Long sleeve tight black top, boot cut denim, and a pair of all black zoom pegasus’ one I’ve sold a million times at Nike.

Her fair skin, tied up black hair.

Unforgettable.

I forgot.
