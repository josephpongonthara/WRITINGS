PLOT POINTS

(1)Studious upbringing from loving, yet orthodox parents. Non violent, passionate & easily manipulated, but boy/girl is too pure to abuse that

(2)Studies high level mathematics, excels individually, fails to form relationships or involve his/herself in community

(3) Not noticeably socially awkward but shy, with an excellent ability for faux empathy

(4)Maintains minimal connections with friends/family, not a recluse, not social, works remotely in a high level, high paying mathematics application related position

(5)Due to inability to form connections with others, spends time solving puzzles, watching films, playing video games. Encounters old calculus textbook & begins to do old practice problems, moves into MATLAB graphing

(6)Begins to make intricate designs on MATLAB, graphing advanced 3-D triple integral functions into unique patterns. Decides to print various designs onto posters & posts them around the city over ads that litter the walls & signs of the grungy city it’s set in

(7)Develops anonymous notoriety with posters & he/she becomes obsessed with creating

(8)Since he/she is anonymous publishers begin to publish his/her work & notoriety grows beyond the city to a national/global scale

(9)Visions of fame & wealth fill his/her mind as they contemplate taking ownership

(10)Due to the artist’s nature, he/she imagines the power to manipulate associated with fame & battles with the pros & cons of taking ownership for his/her work

(11)After discovering that his/her work is making generational wealth they devise a plan to take it for themselves

(12)Individual sues the publishing company for IP theft?(not sure yet exactly)proving that he/she is the creator

(13)Individual wins the lawsuit becoming uber wealthy, but publishing company reveals identity along with individuals plan to stop creating & remove all of their created work from being viewed/profited from

(14)Individual receives immense backlash & hatred, ends all communication with friends & loved ones

(15)Moves to private estate (vineyard?) where he/she models plants in the same form as their MATLAB creations

(16)Kills themselves & new home gets taken over & used as a museum
