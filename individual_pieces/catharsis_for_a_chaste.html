I’m a handsome fellow. Especially recently, so it’s not for lack of opportunity. Beyond my knowing I haven’t turned homosexual; I haven’t gotten a boner from looking at an attractive man yet so that possibility can be ruled out. Religious fanaticism hasn’t taken its hold over me. Another possibility that can be ruled out. For those of you that know me, & if you’re reading this you definitely do, I’m not seeking commitment. I wrote a story a few months back titled ‘Fringe Faggot’. Loosely inspired by the ’The Talented Mr. Ripley’, to shitty to share, & definitely not about a homosexual. Its purpose was to discuss the emotional equivalent of edging. An individual on the cusp of intimacy, choosing to curb the interest of the collective in favor of his/her own. Why? Because there is satisfaction in secrecy. And for those constantly plagued with fear of other’s judgment, it’s a perfect sanctuary to lock up inner desires & enjoy them for yourself without anyone’s approval or condescension. This is where the problem lies. I’ve chosen to set aside all my inherent sexual perversions to lie in this abyss of loneliness. I want to leave, I know I can leave, but I haven’t & don’t know when or if I’ll leave. Why?

I don’t know.
