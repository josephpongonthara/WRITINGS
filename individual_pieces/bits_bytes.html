bits & bytes

Bits & Bytes, I study with fear.
It must be done, I’m sorry my dear.
“Is our date night cancelled?”, she asks with dread.
Her eyes swell with anger, is she gonna fill me with lead?
All the time spent in paranoia, has now turned to hate.
My biggest fear to fruition, she’s becoming irate!
“I love you”, I cry, the words no longer hold weight.
My life cuts too black, I’ve turned into Tony today.
